Sahara

The Sahara is the largest hot desert in the world and the third-largest desert overall, behind the cold deserts of Antarctica and the Arctic. One of the harshest environments on Earth, the Sahara is most famous for its sprawling sand dune fields.

Western Desert

It is characterized by vast expanses of sand dunes, stony plateaus, and rocky terrain.

Atacama Desert

Located in Chile, South America, it's one of the driest deserts on Earth.

Arabian Desert

The Arabian Desert is a vast desert region located in Western Asia.

Thar Desert

Also known as the Great Indian Desert, it spans parts of India and Pakistan.
